PLANT LIFE

Tips and Tricks for your garden

  1. Make sure you know which plants can grow in certain conditions and adjust to be able to care for those plants.

  2. Find out how often each plant needs water and how much it needs.

  3. When taking care of pests try to use natural predators to your benefit before resorting to chemicals.

  4. Test your soil for lead before growing edible plants as you can get sick f the lead level is too high.

  5. Build a trellis it helps to grow many plants and adds to the scenery.

  6. Use compost to help build the nutrients in the soil to add on to your garden.

  7. Try to use plants that will attract pollinators to help grow your garden even further.
