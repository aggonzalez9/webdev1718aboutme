Animals are very interesting. There is many different types of animals that all have different behaviors. They can be dangerous or very peaceful. Many have defenses to protect them from people or other animals. They sometimes have poison defenses such as poisonous to eat or they have a poisonous bite.

Learning about animals can be very useful for many reasons. If you plan to be a biologist knowing about animals is very important. Knowing about animals if you venture in nature is also very important. You can know which animals are dangerous so that you steer away from them. You can also know where certain animals appear to view them. If you plan on getting a pet knowing about animals is also very important.
